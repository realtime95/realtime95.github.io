Rashi Rajguru is a London-based artist working predominantly with image, sound and text. Her influences stem from continuous research into the understanding of objects and how they are defined, how this behaviour further extends to the human narration of situations and events.

Currently working for the Institute of Photography at Falmouth University, Camberwell College of Arts at University of the Arts London and the Global Photographies Network.

One half of Spryte.
